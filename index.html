What is Jaaga Study?

Jaaga Study is a community-driven learning laboratory. We move away from traditional lectures and toward intensive, peer-led sprints that focus on emerging technology and creative expression. We believe the best way to learn is by doing—together. Join our mailing list to be the first to know about our upcoming programs.
