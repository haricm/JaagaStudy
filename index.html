What is Jaaga Study?

Jaaga Study is a community-driven learning laboratory. We move away from traditional lectures and toward intensive, peer-led sprints that focus on emerging technology and creative expression. We believe the best way to learn is by doing—together. Join our mailing list to be the first to know when we launch new cohorts.

How We Learn

Hands-on First

No passive watching; we build from day one.

Peer-to-Peer

Learn alongside a vibrant community of creators.

Online-First

Accessible, virtual programs designed for global collaboration.

Experimental

We play with tools at the bleeding edge, from AI to decentralized tech.
