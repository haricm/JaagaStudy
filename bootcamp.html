Join Discord

Join an experimental nine-week virtual cohort of enthusiasts and artists from January 19th to March 13th. This program serves as an online extension of the Mars College program on Creative AI, focused on hands-on exploration of emerging creative tools.

Why Join This Bootcamp?

This is a free, online, hands-on program designed for those who want to move beyond surface-level AI. Over two months, we follow a simple arc: exploring ideas, developing technical skills, and building projects.

Evolution of Skill

Shift from basic prompting to a more in depth understanding of how the AI tool space for creative work is evolving.

Creative Automation

Learn to scale your output without losing your personal aesthetic.

Review

Interact with experts on Discord and get your work reviewed.

Tools

Receive free credits on eden.art for your projects.

Community

Connect with others interested in the intersection of art and technology.

How it Works

Whether you are a professional artist or just curious, this program is open to anyone with a drive to experiment. There are no technical prerequisites.

Easy Enrollment

To join the cohort, simply sign up by January 18th.

The Experience

This is a self-led journey supported by the community. You will primarily use Eden.Art—a creative toolkit for generating images, video, and audio—to build "Agents" or custom assistants. You will be able to make the best of use of this program by spending five hours a week.

Support

We will have weekly check-ins on Discord to share progress and stay connected.

Expectations

As this is an experimental first version, we encourage you to follow the curriculum and share your work as you go. For those who find a deep rhythm with their projects, there may be future opportunities to explore the work further through residency programs.
