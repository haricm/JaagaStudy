This nine-week virtual cohort is an experimental pilot. From Jan 19th to March 13th, we’ll be testing a virtual extension of the Mars College Creative AI program. We aren’t following a rigid syllabus; we’re here to get hands-on with emerging tools and see what we can build together in a shared, remote space.

Join us in this shared, remote space to see what we can build together.

Program Timeline

Jan 12 - Jan 18 (Week 0)

Ideas Week

Join the discord group, introduce yourself and share what excites you—be it learning goals, ideas, or artistic visions.

Jan 19 - Feb 15 (4 Weeks)

Skill Development

Feb 16 - Feb 22 (Week 5)

Midterm Hackathon

An intensive, multi-day exercise to build something substantial. End the week by presenting your final project concept.

Feb 23 - Mar 15 (3 Weeks)

Final Project Development

Mar 16 (Week 9)

Showcase

Present your achievements to the broader community and explore post-bootcamp collaborations.
